From Temple Flowers to Sustainable Products

⚠️

The Problem

Every day thousands of flowers are offered in temples. After rituals they are discarded and often end up polluting rivers and landfills.

♻️

The Solution

FloraCycle connects temples with recycling vendors who collect these flowers and convert them into eco-friendly products.

🌿

The Impact

Recycled flowers become incense sticks, natural dyes and compost — helping create a sustainable circular ecosystem.
